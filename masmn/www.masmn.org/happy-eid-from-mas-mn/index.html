Happy Eid From MAS MN


Assalamu Alaikum.
Eid Al Adha is on Friday!

Based on the decision of the Fiqh Council of North America, Eid Al Adha is on Friday September 1st, 2017 inshaAllah.

The Muslim American Society of MN congratulates the Muslim Umma for Eid al Adha. We pray that Allah (SWT), showers us with his mercy and bestow his guidance and support on us. Ameen!
