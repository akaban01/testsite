Scholarship Recipient 2005-06

1st Place Grand Prize ($5,000):

Sumaiya Mamdani

  • University of Minnesota - College of Liberal Arts
  • Major: Child Psychology
  • IRG Speaker
  • Al-Amal School Volunteer (Science Fair Judge, Tutor, MC at Graduation)
  • Vice President al-Madinah Cultural Center 2005
  • Advisor MYM and a member of the al-Amal School PR Committee
  • Student Experience Intership, Volunteer Therapist for a child with Cerebral Palsy

2nd Place ($2,000):

Liala Helal

  • University of Minnesota - Journalism and Mass Communications
  • Major: Journalism & Mass Communication and English
  • Minor: Management
  • Journalism Research Assistant and a Reporter with the Minnesota Daily (Campus Newspaper)
  • Arabic Teacher at the ICM Saturday School
  • Excellence in Leadership Award at the U of MN Student Activities Office

3rd Place ($2,000):

Abdul Basit

  • University of Minnesota - Institute of Technology
  • University of MN (M.S.E.E)
  • Event Coordinator and now a President of Al-Madinah Cultural Center
  • Advisor and President of Small World Discussion panel and Coffee Hour

4th Place ($2,000):

Harris Khan

9 week Summer Volunteer work in Costa Rica

Table Leader and Youth Leadership Award for Interfaith Youth Forum started by the Archdiocese of Milwaukee for selected teenage Muslims, jews and Chistians.

  • University of Minnesota - Institute of Technology
  • Major: Biomedical Engineering
  • Volunteer at the University Scholars Community Service Events (Shelter work, Nursing home operations, etc.)

5th Place ($2,000):

Khaled El-Sawaf

  • University of Minnesota - College of Liberal Arts
  • University of Minnesota undergraduate student in College of Liberal Arts, pursuing a degree in political science
  • Plans on going to law school in the future and using his law career to serve the community both with his legal skills and with involvement in politics
  • Treasurer of MSA
  • Member of U of M Parliamentary Traveling Debate Team, competed in National Tournaments.

6th Place ($2,000):

Nasra Giama

  • University of Minnesota - School of Nursing
  • Program Assistant for the School Age Child Care at the Rochester School District#535

  • Represented RCTC MSA at the Rochester International Organization (Rochester World Festival)
